About Me

Designing Stories Through Fabric

With over 10 years of experience in costume design for theater, film, and television, I specialize in creating authentic, character-driven costumes that enhance storytelling.

My approach combines meticulous historical research with innovative textile techniques to produce costumes that are both visually stunning and functionally practical for performers.

I've had the privilege of collaborating with renowned directors on productions ranging from historical dramas to fantasy epics, always striving to create memorable visual identities for characters.
